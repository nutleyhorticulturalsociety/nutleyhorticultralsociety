Outings

High Beeches

Our outing to High Beeches is a shared transport event on Saturday 21st May leaving the Memorial Hall car park at 11 am. Lunch is provided in the cost of the outing. Anyone wishing to join us please contact me or Heather Marcau for further details. Information will also be available at the Spring show on April 2nd.

Bradness Gallery and Gardens

Outing to Bradness Gallery and Gardens, Balcombe, is a shared transport event. Leaving the car park at 10 am on July 31st. Tea and cakes is provided in the cost. Please contact Heather Marcau or Claire Sullivan for further information. If you would like to join us you can also put your name on the list at the Spring show.
