Welcome

We are a long established horticultural society and are affiliated to the Royal Horticultural Society We have three shows a year: the spring show in March, the rose show on village day in June and the autumn show in September. We also have talks by speakers in the Memorial Hall, which are free to members, and outings to gardens.
