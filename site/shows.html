Spring show

Entry forms should be at Ridgefield by 6 pm Thursday before the show. Staging will be from 9.30 to 11 am and open to the public from 2 until 4 pm. Prize giving will be at 3.30 followed by the raffle. There will be refreshments available during the afternoon. Entry to non members is .50p

Rose show

Entry forms should be at Ridgefield by 6 pm Thursday before the show. Staging will be from 9.30 to 11 am and open to the public from 1 until 4 pm. Prize giving will be at 3.30 and will be at the usual tent during the village day event on Ford's Green
