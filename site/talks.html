Talks

Change of regular talk days

This year our talks will take place on a Wednesday evenings at 7.45 instead of the usual Friday evenings at the end of the months of April, September and January and entry to none members has regretably had to be increased to £5.00 per visitor.

Harry Townsend

Harry Townsend is giving us talk on Kew Gardens, Past, present amd future at the Memorial Hall on wednesday 27th April at 7.45. This is free to members and £5.00 for guests. There will also be a raffle and teas at the end.

Patricia Liechti-Clarke

Talk by Patricia Liechti-Clarke on Unusual garden plants at the memorial Hall on september 28th at 7.45 pm. There will also be a raffle and teas afterwards. Entry is free to members and £5.00 to non members.

John Negus and AGM

Talk by John Negus on Perennial Borders follows the AGM on Wednesday 25th January 2017. Ther will be a raffle and teas afterwards. membership renewals will be taken at the start of the events and schedules issued free to all members with their membership.
